Hi! I am a student researcher designing machine learning and bioinformatics pipelines to decipher neurodegeneration. I'm currently in the Institute for Neurodegenerative Disease at Massachusetts General Hospital. Beyond my research endeavors, I'm a photography enthusiast, creative writer, and avid consumer of mangoes.

Some of my most recent work is shown below. If anything piques your interest, I would love to hear from you. Please don't hesitate to reach out!

Neurodegeneration Transcriptomics

Systematic review and meta-analysis of human transcriptomics reveals neuroinflammation, deficient energy metabolism, and proteostasis failure across neurodegeneration.

Heterogeneity of Astrocyte Reaction

Meta-analysis of mouse transcriptomic studies supports a context-dependent astrocyte reaction in acute CNS injury versus neurodegeneration.

Nematode Extracellular Vesicles

Composition of Caenorhabditis elegans extracellular vesicles suggests roles in metabolism, immunity, and aging.
